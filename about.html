ABOUT ME

I'm a graphic designer specialising in branding — but I see myself as a creative that explores many fields. I approach everything I create with intention: nothing is random, and everything needs to serve a purpose.

I value clarity, but I don't like work that feels cold or generic. There's always a layer of meaning, emotion, or narrative behind it. Aesthetically, I'm drawn to refined, contemporary visuals with a strong sense of balance, clean compositions, thoughtful typography, and grounded palettes.

Strong outcomes come from a mix of clear vision, collaboration, and disciplined execution.
